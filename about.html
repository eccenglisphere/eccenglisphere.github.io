About Us

English Conversation Club (ECC) is an extracurricular activity designed to help students improve their English speaking and listening skills through regular conversations, discussions, and interactive activities. The club provides a supportive and enjoyable environment where members can practice English in real-life contexts, build confidence, and enhance their communication abilities.
